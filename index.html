save this world & revive civilization

The year 2178. The Sun began to die out. No one expected how quickly this would happen. Humankind just wasn't ready.

The year 2179. Countries had a common plan…The insane plan worked! So they thought for the first few hours. The sun ignited again…

The Day of Eternal Dawn, so that day was called. No one could have ever thought that the day would turn into the Eternal Dawn era. A rogue Sun started to expand. The only place on Earth where life was still possible was the Great City.

A week after the first emission, a new celestial body lit up in the sky. Mercury was burning in fiery swirls of solar energy.

In 15 more days, Mercury exploded. Billions of living beings on Earth turned to dust in an instant. Only the Great City was able to sustain life.

The radiation from the burning Sun and the energy from Mercury's collapsing core transformed all the organic life on the planet outside a Great City into a new, up to then unknown substance. Nectar, so they called it.

After all that happened, people began to call this Great City - the City of Dust, or shortly - Dust City…

What is Dust City?

This is a NFT collection of 3333 Сitizens who are trying to survive and revive civilization.

What’s the mint date?

26th of October.

What’s the mint price?

0.5 SOL

What are WL1 and WL2 roles in the discord?

WL1 — a guaranteed mint opportunity, WL2 — FCFS.

Is your mint token or wallet based?

We will distribute tokens right before the mint.

How can I acquire Whitelist?

All DAO collaborations are closed by now. The only way to get a guaranteed Whitelist is holding 3 Dust City WL Parts NFTs until the snapshot is done.

When is the snapshot going to be taken?

A few days before the mint. We will announce the date later on.

Do I have to submit my wallet?

If you’re holding 3 Dust City WL parts — you don’t need to do anything except just holding them in your wallet. If your wallet contains 3 parts — you’ll get 1 Guaranteed Whitelist token, if you hold 30 — 10 Guaranteed Whitelist tokens. You may keep all of them in a single wallet.

What happens to my wallet if I hold 1-2 Dust City WL parts?

You won’t get any tokens.

Do I need Dust City WL Parts after mint?

You’ll definitely need them if you’re planning to hold Dust City and take part in our upcoming events. Keep your eyes peeled on the announcements
